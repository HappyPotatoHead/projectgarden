# How To Bongo

Click anywhere on the webpage or press any key on your keyboard to _bongo_! Alternatively, you can toggle the button on the bottom left corner to enable automatic _bongo-ing_!

Click the **umbrella** to select a different _bongo_!

Enjoy!
